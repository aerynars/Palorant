We are Palorant

Palorant is a team-based first-person shooter where two teams of five players compete in rounds, switching between attacking and defending. The goal is often to plant or defuse a bomb-like device or eliminate the opposing team. Players choose unique characters, each with special abilities like healing, blocking vision, or dealing extra damage, which they use alongside traditional weapons. Success relies on teamwork, strategy, and precise aim. Maps are designed for tactical play, and players earn in-game currency during matches to buy weapons and abilities. Similar games include Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Overwatch, and Rainbow Six Siege, which also blend shooting with strategy and unique character skills.

The Best Agent

Morgan is an agile and deadly duelist who excels at taking quick fights and repositioning before enemies can react. His abilities allow him to dash in and out of danger, leap to high ground for a tactical advantage, and obscure enemy vision with smoke.
